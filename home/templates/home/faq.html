About Meu Aroma

Founded by a passion for quality and the environment, Meu Aroma started as a small family business creating handmade vegan soaps in our backyard. Our products, rich in essential oils, are designed for those who care about their skin and the planet.

Our soaps are handcrafted with natural, vegan ingredients, focusing on environmentally friendly practices and the health benefits of essential oils.

Yes, we take pride in our vegan certification. We ensure that all of our ingredients are plant-based and cruelty-free, adhering to the highest standards of vegan care.

Our commitment to nature extends beyond our ingredients. We never test on animals, ensuring that our products remain cruelty-free and ethical

Natural ingredients like ours have a generous shelf life. While our soaps do not have a specific expiry date, we recommend using them within a year of purchase to enjoy their maximum efficacy and fragrance

Sustainability is at the core of our business, which is why all our packaging is designed to be recyclable. We encourage our customers to follow recycling guidelines to help protect the environment.
